Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

LalaRetro ("we", "our", "the app") is a cosmetic ingredient tracking app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

What data we collect

LalaRetro stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Products you add (name, brand, ingredients, barcode)
  • Skin reactions you log (products used, reaction types, dates, notes)
  • Your ingredient watchlist

Where your data is stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. If you have iCloud enabled, your data is also synced via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store so it is available across your devices. We do not operate any servers. Your data never leaves Apple's ecosystem.

Camera and photo library

LalaRetro requests camera access to scan barcodes and photo library access to select photos of ingredient lists for OCR text recognition. Photos are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No images are uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

Analytics and tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or behavioral data. We do not share any data with third parties.

Health data

Skin reaction data you log is stored only on your device and iCloud account. It is not shared with anyone. LalaRetro is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice.

Data deletion

You can delete individual products and reactions within the app. To delete all data, uninstall the app and remove LalaRetro data from your iCloud account via Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.

Children's privacy

LalaRetro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will post the changes here with a new "last updated" date.
